Together we're securing plastic out of the biosphere to make building blocks, brikcoins and green spaces.

GoBrik is a ecological contribution platform that provides ecobrickers and their communities with the tools to log, track, exchange and manage their ecobricking while rewarding their service to the biosphere with with Brikcoins.
